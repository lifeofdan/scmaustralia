About Us

Southern Cross Ministries was established in 2002. In 2003, Jim, Becky, Daniel and Andrew Newman moved to Toowoomba Australia. The purpose of Southern Cross Ministries is to plant churches and train leaders in Australia. This ministry works in partnership with churches and individuals in Australia and the USA to accomplish these goals.

The local church group that Southern Cross Ministries works with is called the Eastern Downs Church of Christ. It is located in the "Darling Downs" region of South East Queensland, near the city of Tooowoomba. The Darling Downs is home to over 250,000 residents and is both an educational center and rich agricultural area.
